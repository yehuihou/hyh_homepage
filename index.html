About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am a theoretical astrophysicist specializing in black hole physics and images.

I obtained my Ph.D. from Peking University and my Bachelor's degree from Beihang University.

Currently, my research focuses on the polarization patterns of supermassive black holes in the millimeter wavelength; the accretion, jet, magnetic fields, and particle dynamics at the event horizon scale.

You can find more about my work on my Inspire profile. Feel free to contact me at yehuihou@pku.edu.cn.
